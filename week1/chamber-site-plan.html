Site Name

This site is named Floripa Chamber of Commerce. It represents the local area where the chamber operates, fostering business growth and community development.

Site Purpose

The Floripa Chamber of Commerce website serves to:

  • Promote local businesses through member services and networking events.
  • Provide resources for economic development and community growth initiatives.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #3B6E98
Secondary Color: rgb(160, 179, 211)

#3B6E98 will be used for headings and accents, while rgb(160, 179, 211) will be used for backgrounds and borders.

Typography

Main Font: Montserrat
Secondary Font: Open Sans

Montserrat will be used for headings and Open Sans for body text.
